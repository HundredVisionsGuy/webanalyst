About BTS

BTS, which stands for *Bangtan Sonyeondan in Korean and Beyond the Scene in English, is a South Korean music group with seven members. They made their debut on June 12, 2013 from the company Big Hit Entertainment. The members are:

  • RM
  • Jin
  • Suga
  • J-Hope
  • Jimin
  • V
  • Jungkook

In the beginning, the group was not respected in the K-Pop industry because they came from a small company. They did not have the influence to gain respect by the media or industry.

But, BTS began to rise to fame in South Korea after they finally won their first music show award. There are a variety of Korean shows that allow musicians to promote and perform their new songs. Thus, when they placed first on The Show for "I Need U", it was momentous for them.

Then, BTS gained popularity in the Western music market, especially the United States, with "DNA" in 2017. This led them to perform at their first American award show: the American Music Awards. Also, they became the first Asian act to win Top Social Artist at the Billboard Music Awards.

BTS are known for their performances, lyrics, and fictional storyline (BTS Universe).

They are considered self-producing idols. Although they are not always involved in the creation process, they do participate in most of it. Either for the group or solo work, all of the members have helped

Fans of the group are called *ARMY. Because of ARMY's dedication and passion, the group has been able to reach success. They are known to

  • Stream and buy music
  • Promote BTS
  • Organize fundraisers in BTS' name

*Bangtan Sonyeondan= Bulletproof Boy Scouts

Campaigns

Love Myself

In 2017, BTS began their Love Yourself series, which comprised of three albums (Love Yourself: Her, Love Yourself: Tear, & Love Yourself: Answer) and one video (Love Yourself: Wonder). Each album journeyed through different types of love:

  • Happy love
  • Painful love
  • Self-love

The aim of the series was to tell a story about finding and exploring true love, which was realized to be self-love.

The message was to love yourself because accepting your worth and flaws will help you love others. When you can love yourself, you can give that same love to others.

On November 1, 2017, BTS and Big Hit Music partnered with UNICEF (United Nations Children's Fund) in their #ENDviolence campaign in order to spread the message of self-love. #ENDviolence's objective was to eradicate violence against children and teens by providing them safety. It was created to promote protection for young people and devise solutions to end violence.

Thus, the group and their label created the LOVE MYSELF campaign in partnership with UNICEF. LOVE MYSELF promoted self-love for young children globally. With love, the world can become a better place to live in.

To raise awareness, BTS promoted the cause on social media. At some concerts, UNICEF and LOVE MYSELF booths were set up to teach fans about the cause's message. Fans have contributed to the cause by

  • Donating money
  • Spreading awareness with #BTSLoveMyself
  • Sharing their personal self-love journeys

BTS and Big Hit Music pledged to donate 3% of the Love Yourself album series and all the proceeds made from the campaign's merchandise. Donations are made towards programs supporting and protecting children and teens from:

  • Domestic violence
  • School violence
  • Sexual violence

As of October 2021, $3.6 million was raised thanks to BTS, Big Hit Music and ARMY.

Achievements

BTS has made history as artists and Asians with the awards and records they achieved For example, the South Korean government honored them with the Minister of Culture, Sports and Tourism Commendation in 2016. It was for their contribution in pop culture.

Some of the awards and honors they achieved were:

  • 2021: Artist of the Year at the American Music Awards
  • 2021: Special Presidential Envoy for Future Generations and Culture
  • 2020: James A. Van Fleet Award
  • 2020: Time Entertainer of the Year
  • 2019: Album of the Year at Seoul Music Awards for Love Yourself: Tear
  • 2019: Global Sustainable Future Leaders
  • 2018: Hangwon Order of Cutural Merit (5th class)
  • 2018: Best Music Video at Mnet Asian Awards for "Idol"
  • 2017: Song of the Year at Melon Music Awards for "Spring Day"
  • 2017: Album of the Year for 4th Quarter for Wings at Gaon Chart Music Awards
  • 2016: Album of the Year at Melon Music Awards for The Most Beautiful Moment in Life: Young Forever
  • 2015: World Rookie Award at Gaon Chart Music Awards
  • 2014: Best New Artist at Seoul Music Awards
